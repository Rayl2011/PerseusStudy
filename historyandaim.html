Aims

The Perseus' Study group is eager to welcome you and support your academic journey in a warm, respectful, and nurturing way.
By leveraging their diverse range of free (as in free beer) resources, including online lessons, comprehensive study materials, and collaborative discussion forums, they are well-equipped to help you overcome any obstacles you may face, while also fostering a sense of community and camaraderie among its members.
The group also encourages the development of interests outside the traditional academic realm, such as music, gaming, technology, and coding, ensuring a truly holistic and fulfilling learning experience.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive academic support to help individuals overcome any educational challenges they may face. We offer a wide range of personalized services, including interactive online lessons, detailed study materials, and collaborative discussions facilitated by subject matter experts, all designed to empower students, remove barriers to learning, and help them achieve their full potential through the cultivation of academic mastery, critical thinking skills, problem-solving abilities, and a lifelong love of learning.
We believe information should be always free and easy to access. This is the key.
